Child Safety Standards & Policies

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

FlirtMap is committed to providing a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all users within our location-based community platform. We maintain an absolute zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child exploitation, abuse, or endangerment.

1. Absolute Zero-Tolerance Policy

FlirtMap strictly prohibits the generation, uploading, sharing, or transmission of any Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (CSAE). There are no exceptions to this rule. Any attempt to use our platform for such purposes will result in immediate, severe, and irreversible restrictive actions.

2. Active Moderation and Reporting Mechanisms

To ensure compliance and maintain community safety, FlirtMap implements the following active moderation systems:

  • User Reporting Tools: Every message, post, or "shout" on the map includes an accessible and visible reporting mechanism, allowing users to flag inappropriate content immediately.
  • Priority Review: Any report categorized or flagged under child safety concerns is automatically escalated to our priority moderation queue for immediate human review.
  • Proactive Automated Checks: We utilize specialized detection filters within our database architecture to identify and flag suspicious patterns or text strings related to prohibited content.

3. Immediate Enforcement and Permanent Device Banning

Upon detection or validation of a child safety violation, FlirtMap enforces instantaneous punitive measures:

  • Content Removal: The offending content is permanently and immediately deleted from our live servers and databases.
  • Hardware-Level Ban: The specific Device ID / Installation ID associated with the offender is permanently blacklisted. This prevents the offender from accessing the application, posting new content, or creating alternative anonymous profiles from that specific terminal.

4. Full Cooperation with Law Enforcement Authorities

FlirtMap operates in strict compliance with local and international legal standards. In accordance with our policies:

  • We actively preserve necessary metadata and logs related to validated severe violations.
  • We cooperate fully and transparently with law enforcement agencies, cybercrime units, and relevant child protection organizations (such as NCMEC or equivalent national authorities) to assist in the identification and prosecution of offenders.

5. Designated Point of Contact

Our designated safety officer has the required training, authority, and capabilities to handle inquiries regarding child safety prevention practices, compliance, and enforcement reports.
